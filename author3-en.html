About the Author

Jassim Mohammed Ali Al-Maamouri is an Iraqi writer, storyteller, and poet interested in literature in general, and in short stories and narrative arts in particular. He has written in literary criticism, politics, religion, and cultural thought.

His articles, studies, and essays have been published in newspapers and Arabic websites, and he has written under different pen names, including “Abu Sajjad Al-Maamouri” and “Jassim Al-Maamouri”.

He believes that writing is among the highest forms of intellectual and human expression, capable of shaping awareness, encouraging reflection, and creating meaningful change within society.

His writing style often combines literary language with reflective thought, with a special focus on storytelling as a way to understand human experience and its deeper transformations.
