Who Are We?

Al-Hiwar Al-Thaqafi (The Cultural Dialogue) is an open space for thought, expression, and the exchange of ideas between writers and readers. This website is designed to gather voices, publish articles, and offer content that reflects the richness of human experience.

Our Mission

We believe that culture is a right for everyone — not just a select few. Through this platform, we aim to provide high-quality, honest, and meaningful content that resonates with readers and enriches their understanding.

Our Goals

  • Supporting new writers by giving them a space to publish.
  • Collecting cultural and intellectual articles in one place.
  • Promoting dialogue and respect for different viewpoints.
  • Offering a simple, clean, and accessible reading experience.

Why Was This Website Created?

Because we believe that words can inspire change, and that dialogue is the foundation of cultural and intellectual development in every society.
